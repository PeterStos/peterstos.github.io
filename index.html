    About me

    I am a funny husband, a proud father, and an active member of the community.

    Looking back at my life I can conclude I am a determined and adaptable person. I can explain why. Growing up, I was never a great swimmer, or a runner or very much into bike rides. But I always had two dreams: participating in open water races and run a marathon. With small hands, narrow shoulders, and zero experience as an athlete I started small, but never gave up on practicing. After a few months I was not only collecting triathlon finisher medals but also finished a marathon. Might not sound a great accomplishment, but it requires a considerable commitment and discipline.

    After working for more than 8 years as a Civil Engineer in huge hydroelectric projects, I decided it was time for a new challenge. I turned my life upside down and moved with my family to another country, with no job, but with a new aspiration: work with software development. Since then I have been spending my free time coding, studying new technologies and developing new skills.
